Night Market

Is the place to go for those who love fashion and cheap things, visit the baguio night market and you will definitely like it because of many product, if you want to shop for cheap things, you have a lot to choose from at the night market without burning a hole in your wallet

Burnham Park

Burnham Park is an open green park at the heart of Baguio. It's nice to have a picnic here, you will definitely enjoy the activities they have such as boating, biking and there are also many dancing zomba and everyone can participate.

Valley of Colors

Valley of Colors these La Trinidad houses were turned into huge, vibrant artwork and now considered as one of the newest tourist attraction near Baguio City. I highly recommend you to see its beauty and colorful houses because it’s so Instagrammable!

Mines View

Mines View Park is the place to go if you want wide and mountainous views, it's good to go to mine view, because the atmosphere is very relaxing, especially for those who are stressed and who just want to relax and go on vacation

The Botanical Garden

The Botanical garden is a place where you can see towering pine trees, colorful flowers, and many types of plants, as well as indigenous huts, sculptures, and relics of the Igorot people. it is perfect for photoshoots like wedding photoshoot, debut etc. because of the very colorful view.

The Mansion

The Mansion is one of the most popular tourist spots in Baguio City. This is the official summer residence of Philippine. Getting a glance at its magnificent palace and wide lush green garden is quite pleasant.

The Mirador Heritage

Mirador Park is one of the newest tourist spot in Baguio City. It has different attractions and features. It has rock garden formation, bamboo groves, gardens, Nipa huts and peace memorial. It is also the best place to visit if you want peace and reflections. Witness this breathtaking view and spend time to have your inner peace and reflections.

Strawberry Farm

Strawberry Farm in La Trinidad is a big strawberry farm where you can get and pick a fresh strawberries by yourself. Picking fresh strawberries is one of the most awesome experiences, isn't it exciting? The best season to pick strawberries is from November to May.

Foods

Sundot kulangot

Often sold in packs, this bundle of joy is made to try for every sweet-tooth. Packed inside tiny wooden shells, the combination of sticky rice and brown sugar created this appetizing treat. Its savory taste might even make you forget to ask what its name is in English.

Peanut Brittle

Here in the Philippines, peanut brittle is a popular "pasalubong" (souvenir) from a place called Baguio…..

Strawberry Taho

Strawberry Taho is a famous delicacy in Baguio City. It's a soybean curd with strawberry sauce and pieces. With this recipe, you don't need to travel all the way to Baguio just to taste their famous treat.

Strawberry jam

The best-selling fruit preserve goes well with so many things that it’s best to buy more than a single jar at a time. Some of our favorite foods to pair with strawberry jam include bread, crackers, yogurt, crepes, and smoothies.

Sinanglaw

Sinanglaw is a Filipino soup dish prepared with beef and beef offal flavoured with kamias and bile, nearly similar to pinapaitan.
